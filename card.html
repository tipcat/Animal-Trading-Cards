Yellow Mongoose

Reknowned killers of cobra snakes, owing to their exceptional speed and immunity to snake venom.

  • Scientific Name: Cynictis penicillata
  • Average Length: 20 inches (500 mm)
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 15 yrs (in captivity)
  • Habitat: Southern African grasslands & scrub land

This variety of mongoose is also known as the Red Meerkat. They live in colonies of up to 20 and will often cohabit with squirrels.
